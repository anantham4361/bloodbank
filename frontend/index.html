Benefits of Blood Donation

Saves Lives

One donation can save up to three lives by helping patients in emergencies.

Health Check-Up

Get a free mini check-up of your blood pressure, hemoglobin level, and more.

Burns Calories

Donating blood burns about 650 calories per pint. Stay healthy while helping others.

Who Can Donate?

  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • Minimum weight of 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin level at least 12.5 g/dL
  • Not suffering from infectious diseases
  • Should not have donated blood in the last 3 months
